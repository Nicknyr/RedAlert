Community

Getting Help

If you run into problems, your first stop should be the OpenRA FAQ.

If that doesn't help, you should get in touch (see below) or file a bug on the OpenRA Bug Tracker.

Getting in Touch

The best means of contacting us is via our irc channel, #openra on freenode.

The Freenode web client is easy to use if you don't have an IRC client.

You can also try our forum, ModDB page, Twitter, Facebook, Google+ or Reddit.

Getting Involved

We are a small team, so anything you can do to help is greatly appreciated.

See the development topics on our wiki for more information.

Getting Content

Share custom content such as maps on the OpenRA Resource Center site.

Replays, strategies and videos can be found at the GameReplays.org OpenRA section.
